About

A career of
environmental storytelling.

I'm Emmett Gartner, a freelance climate and environment reporter based in Queens, NY. Having grown up on the Chesapeake Bay, I've long been followed stories of adaptation and accountability.

I spent the past three years as a staff writer for The Maine Monitor reporting on how government policies helped or failed Maine communities grappling with the consequences of pollution and climate change

Previously, I reported for the Frederick News-Post in western Maryland and witnessed first-hand how climate change is influencing drought and wildfires as a trail maintenance worker in Nevada and Oregon.

My work has been published by the Boston Globe, Washington Post, Associated Press, and more. I'm available for long-form investigative reporting, feature writing, and quicker turnaround assignments.
